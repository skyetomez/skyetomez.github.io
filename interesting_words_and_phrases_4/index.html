Interesting Words and Phrases 4

  • Aggrandize - Increase the power, status, or wealth of. Enhance the reputation of (someone) beyond what is justified by the facts.
  • Bereave - Be deprived of a loved one through a profound absence especially due to the loved one's death.
  • Mania - Mental illness marked by periods of great excitement or euphoria, delusions, and overactivity. An excessive enthusiasm or desire; obsession.
  • Chagrin - Distress or embarrassment at having failed or been humiliated.
  • Valiant - Possessing or showing courage or determination.
  • Futile - Incapable of producing any useful result; pointless.
  • Acedia - Spiritual or mental sloth; apathy.
  • Ascetic - Characterized by or suggesting the practice of sever self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.
  • Sad - feeling or showing sorrow; unhappy.
